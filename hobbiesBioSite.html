Hobbies

Metaphysics

Metaphysics was considered a science back then but is now a branch of philosophy. To put it simply, it explores our reality, our existence, what we are beyond our physical bodies. Abstract Concepts like time, space, and human identity. Metaphysics explores a question like "Do humans have souls?" and "What are the components of a reality?".

Meditation

Meditation is practice that involves clearing the mind. Clearing your mind is much easier said than done; as it is not only about clearing the mind but being present in the moment. During mediation, you are not thinking of yourself but of being in the moment without analyitical thinking.

Creation

Whether it be cooking, doing hair, the ability to create is a hobby she also enjoys. Particularly, creating jewelry as a form of art she finds meditative. Using feathers from nature, pouring her time and energy hand crafting each piece for each earring she creates. The act becomes a ritual of mindfulness and the finished piece is not only a beautiful reflection of the soul but a reminder of inner peace and purpose.
